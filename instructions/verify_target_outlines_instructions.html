How to perform the task

  1. Read the instructions below and make sure you understand them.
  2. Examine the diagram carefully and answer the following question: is the part of the diagram that the text refers to outlined according to the instructions?
  3. If the part is outlined according to the instructions, choose Yes (or press 1).
    If not, choose No (or press 2).
  4. Click Submit to submit the completed tasks.
To pass the training, 70% of your answers need to be correct.
To pass the examination, 80% of your answers need to be correct.

Payment information: You will be paid $0.035 per assignment. You will be paid immediately after the work is completed.

Purpose statement: These tasks are part of a research project conducted at the University of Helsinki.

Instructions

You will be shown outlines for diagram elements that contain text, letters or numbers and the target related to them.

You must check if the outlines of the target(s) that the source refers to have been drawn according to the instructions below.
