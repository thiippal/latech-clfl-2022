How to perform the task

  1. Read the instructions below and make sure you understand them.
  2. Examine the image carefully and locate text, letters and numbers. If necessary, use the zoom tool to make sure you see the whole diagram.
  3. Some of the text, letters and numbers may be already outlined. Outline only the elements that are not already outlined. 
  4. Use the rectangle (hotkey: W) or polygon (hotkey: E) tool to draw accurate outlines for all diagram elements that contain text, letters and numbers.
  5. When you have outlined all elements that contain text, letters and numbers, press Submit to submit your work.

Payment information: You will be paid $0.4 per assignment. You will be paid immediately after your work has been verified by other users.

Purpose statement: These tasks are part of a research project conducted at the University of Helsinki.

Instructions

Your task is to locate and outline text, letters or numbers in diagrams from school textbooks.
