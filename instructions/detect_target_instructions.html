How to perform the task

  1. Read the instructions below and make sure you understand them.
  2. Examine the diagram image carefully. To view the diagram in detail, click the image to enlarge it.
  3. If the text refers to another part of the diagram, choose Yes (or press 1).
    If the text does not refer to any part of the diagram, choose No (or press 2).
  4. Click Submit to submit the completed tasks.
To pass the training, 70% of your answers need to be correct.
To pass the examination, 80% of your answers need to be correct.

Payment information: You are paid $0.17 for taking the examination. You will be paid immediately after the work is completed.

Purpose statement: These tasks are part of a research project conducted at the University of Helsinki.

Instructions

You will be shown outlines for diagram elements that contain text, letters or numbers.

You need to check if the outlined text refers to another part of the diagram.
