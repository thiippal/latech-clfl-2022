How to perform the task:

  1. Examine the diagram image carefully.
  2. To view the diagram in detail, click the image to enlarge it. If the image is sideways or upside down, use the Rotate button.
  3. If the diagram contains text, letters or numbers, choose Yes (or press 1). If the diagram does not contain text or letters, choose No (or press 2).
  4. Click Submit to submit the completed tasks.
To pass the training 70% of your answers need to be correct. 
To pass the exam 80% of your answers need to be correct.

Payment information: You will be paid $0.03 per assignment. You will be paid immediately after the work is completed.

Purpose statement: These tasks are part of a research project conducted at the University of Helsinki.

Examples

In the task, you will see images of diagrams from school textbooks and other learning materials.

You need to check if the diagram contains text, letters or numbers.
